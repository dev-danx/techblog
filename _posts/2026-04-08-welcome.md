This is my first blog post on GitHub Pages!

## Getting Started

Here are the steps to create your own blog:

1. Fork this repository
2. Customize `_config.yml`
3. Add your posts to `_posts/`
4. Push to `main` branch
5. Your blog will be live at `https://yourusername.github.io`

## Writing Posts

Posts are written in Markdown and saved in the `_posts` folder with the format:

```
YYYY-MM-DD-your-title.md
```

Happy blogging! 📝
